About the Data Explorer

The NISRA Data Explorer is a website that provides an interactive way to explore, visualise, and download data from the NISRA Data Portal.

The NISRA Data Portal is the central repository for storing and disseminating NISRA's official statistics. It contains datasets across seven statistical and research themes:

  • People and communities
  • Health and social care
  • Work, pay and benefits
  • Education and skills
  • Transport, environment and climate change
  • Crime and justice
  • Business, economy and trade

The NISRA Data Portal provides open, machine-readable data to support transparency, reuse, and innovation.

What is the Data Explorer?

The Data Explorer is a tool designed to make it easier to:

  • Search and filter datasets published by NISRA and stored on the NISRA Data Portal
  • Create quick visualisations (charts, maps, tables) and download them
  • Export customised views of the data for further analysis
  • Preview and download the dataset

This website is part of our ongoing work to improve access to official statistics and support innovation in how data is used and understood.

Key Features

  • Live connection to the NISRA Data Portal
    The Explorer pulls data directly from the Portal, ensuring users always access the most up-to-date information.
  • Flexible filtering and visualisation
    Users can explore datasets by geography, time, and topic, and view results as charts, maps, or tables.
  • Download options
    Unfiltered data can be downloaded in CSV format for reuse in other tools.

Training and Support for the Data Portal

The Data Explorer is designed to make accessing NISRA's data easier—but it may not meet everyone's needs. If you want to explore the data in more depth, build your own queries, create custom charts or widgets, or use the APIs, we recommend going directly to the NISRA Data Portal.

To help you get started, we've created a User Guide that includes:

  • Step-by-step instructions
  • Video tutorials
  • Tips for making the most of the Portal's features
